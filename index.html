About Us

Royal Media Services is a leading media company offering a wide range of services including broadcasting, digital media, and content production. We have been serving our clients for over 20 years with excellence and innovation.

Services

Our broadcasting services cover radio and television programming, delivering quality content to a wide audience.

Our most popular stations are; Ramogi TV, Citizen TV and Inooro TV.

Royal Media Services offers a wide range of services in the media industry. One of our primary services is television broadcasting, where we operate multiple television channels that cover news, entertainment, and educational content. We also have a strong presence in radio broadcasting, owning and managing various radio stations that deliver a diverse range of programming including news, music, and talk shows.

In addition to traditional broadcasting, Royal Media Services excels in digital media production. We specialize in creating high-quality digital content such as videos, podcasts, and interactive media. Their expertise extends to advertising and marketing, providing comprehensive solutions for businesses looking to reach our target audience through various media channels.

Royal Media Services is also well-known for its event coverage services. We offer live broadcasting, video production, and media partnership for conferences, concerts, and other special events. With a dedicated team of journalists and reporters, we provide comprehensive news coverage, both locally and internationally.

Moreover, Royal Media Services is actively involved in content distribution, making our media content available through television, radio, online streaming, and social media platforms. We also engage in content syndication, allowing other media outlets to distribute their programming and news coverage. Beyond our core services, Royal Media Services provides media training programs and consultancy services, helping individuals and organizations enhance their media skills and strategies. They are committed to corporate social responsibility as well, actively supporting community projects and promoting social welfare through their media platforms.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Royal Media Services puts a lot of emphasis to Corporate Social Responsibility

Cancer Fundrasing Walk

Due to the rise in the current cancer cases in Meru, Muuga FM partners with Meru Hospice during their cancer awareness campaign. It is usually held during the Cancer Month October every year.

Muuga FM Farmer's forum

Our aim is to dedicate the day to farmers; equip them with more innovative farming methods and farming products. This is our way of giving back to the community and supporting food security which is among the big four agenda .We consider this a CSR activity for the station.
